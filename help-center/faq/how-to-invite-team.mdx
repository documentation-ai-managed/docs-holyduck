title: How do I invite my team?
description: Quickly add your team to HolyDuck.
---

## Overview

Invite team members to collaborate on your docs.

## Steps

1. Click your workspace name in the sidebar.
2. Select "Team Settings."
3. Click "Invite Member."
4. Enter their email address.
5. Click "Send Invite."

They will receive an email with an invitation link.
