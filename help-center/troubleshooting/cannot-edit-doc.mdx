---
title: Cannot Edit a Document
description: Fix issues preventing document edits.
---

## Symptoms

- "Read only" message appears
- Edit button is locked

## Common Causes

- You do not have edit permissions
- The doc is locked by another member

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your role">
    Ensure you have "Editor" or "Owner" permissions in Team Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unlock document">
    Ask the current editor to save and close the document, then retry.
  </Step>
</Steps>
