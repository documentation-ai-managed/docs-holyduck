## Overview

Create and publish your first document with HolyDuck in minutes.

## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document">
    Click the + button at the top left and select "New Document."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content">
    Enter your title and body. Use Markdown for formatting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save & Publish">
    Click "Save" to draft, then "Publish" to make it live to your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
