## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
You need a web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge) and an email address for account verification. No software installation required.
</Callout>

## Sign Up for an Account

Follow these steps to create your HolyDuck account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit HolyDuck" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to the [HolyDuck signup page](https://app.holydocs.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA if prompted.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">
      Use a business email for team collaborations.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox and click the verification link. This enables full access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up a space to organize your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="home">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, select visibility (Private or Public), and choose a color scheme.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create**. You now land in your new space's editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Your First Document

Add content to your space using these methods.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="File Upload" icon="upload">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Drag and Drop">
        Drag files (Markdown, PDF, images) into the editor.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Files">
        Click the upload icon and choose from your device.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Paste Content" icon="clipboard">
    Copy text or Markdown from elsewhere and paste directly. HolyDuck auto-formats it.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import from GitHub" icon="github">
    Connect your repo via **Integrations** > **GitHub** and sync Markdown files automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
